# Privacy Policy for Get Your Life Right (GYLR)

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

Get Your Life Right ("GYLR", "we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we access:
- Your email address (for identification)
- Your name (for personalization)
- Your Google Calendar events (titles, dates, times)

### Calendar Data
We read your Google Calendar events to:
- Display them in the app
- Allow you to categorize them
- Calculate time allocation statistics

### Category Preferences
We store your event categorization preferences locally on your device to:
- Remember how you've categorized similar events
- Provide smart category suggestions

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
- All calendar data processing happens on your device
- Event categorizations are stored locally
- Time allocation calculations are performed locally

### AI-Generated Content
When you request a "roast" (comedic judgment):
- Your time allocation percentages (not individual events) are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI
- The AI generates a humorous response based on category percentages only
- No specific event details are shared with the AI

### What We DO NOT Do
- We do not store your data on our servers
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not share individual event details with any third party
- We do not use your data for advertising

## Third-Party Services

### Google
- We use Google OAuth for authentication
- We access Google Calendar API to read your events
- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Anthropic (Claude AI)
- We use Claude AI to generate comedic content
- Only aggregated time percentages are shared (e.g., "Work: 45%")
- Anthropic's Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using:
- AsyncStorage for preferences and categorizations
- Secure storage for authentication tokens

### Data Retention
- Your data remains on your device until you:
  - Sign out of the app
  - Clear app data
  - Delete the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the app
- **Delete**: Clear all stored data via Settings > Clear Category Mappings
- **Revoke**: Remove app access via Google Account settings
- **Uninstall**: Delete the app and all associated data

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Secure token storage for authentication
- HTTPS for all API communications
- No server-side storage of personal data

## Children's Privacy

GYLR is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
